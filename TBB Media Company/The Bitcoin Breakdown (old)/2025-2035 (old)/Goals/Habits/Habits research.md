We are what we repeatedly do  
Therefore excellence is not an act, but a habit
   

[5 Habits That Will Make Your Average Day Happier](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=plZymgBEC4c)
 
- Give yourself time to tinker, explore, and play
    - Find your bliss
    - Learn something
    - Don't be a robot
    - Schedule time to do whatever you want
- Stay physically active and make that activity fun
    - This means listening to podcasts or music while I run or work out
    - Also maybe boxing once a week
    - Taking walks around the city
- Minimize things in your daily life that you don't like
    - Think to yourself, what do I do every day that kind of annoys me
    - Either remove them from your life or optimize to make them more bearable
    - For me this would probably be useless meetings, maybe start declining ones I don't need to go to, going in but muting, or make them better by using the time to be productive with something else, like start scheduling to do items during a meeting
- Take an occasional big break
    - Need to break out of routine
    - Need a mental reset
    - Maybe a digital detox
- Prioritize face to face interactions
    - Don't chase after vanity metrics like likes on fb
    - Digital friendships are nice but they don't compare
