## 🟧 BITCOIN BEST PRACTICES

### What Every Bitcoiner Must Know

**Protect your coins. Preserve your freedom. Reject scams.**
 
### 🔐 SELF-CUSTODY IS NON-NEGOTIABLE

- **Not your keys, not your coins.** If you don’t hold the private keys, you don’t own Bitcoin.
- Use a **hardware wallet** (e.g., Coldcard, Passport, Trezor, BitBox) or secure software wallet (e.g., Sparrow, BlueWallet, Phoenix).
- Avoid storing funds on exchanges, custodial wallets, or apps with flashy logos and too-good-to-be-true promises. 
### 🚨 COMMON SCAMS TO AVOID

- **Impersonators:** No legit Bitcoiner will DM you promising gains, giveaways, or "doubling" your coins.
- **Airdrops & Altcoins:** 99% are pump-and-dump schemes or centralized distractions.
- **Seed Phrase Phishing:** Never enter your seed phrase into a website, app, or share it with _anyone_.
- **Hardware Wallet Scams:** Only buy from official sources. Never use pre-seeded devices. 
### 🔑 SEED PHRASE = YOUR LIFELINE

- Write your seed **offline** on paper or steel. Never screenshot, photograph, or type it into cloud services.
- Store backups in secure, geographically separate locations.
- Consider multisig for larger holdings or inheritance planning. 
### 🧠 STAY SOVEREIGN

- Use **Bitcoin-only** wallets. Avoid wallets that support altcoins—they often have poor privacy and security practices.
- Learn to use **coin control** and avoid address reuse.
- Run your own **Bitcoin full node**. Trust yourself, not third parties. 
### 🛰️ PRIVACY ISN’T A CRIME — IT’S A NECESSITY

- Use **Tor** or **VPN** when transacting.
- Avoid KYC services if possible. Once your identity is tied to your Bitcoin, surveillance becomes easy.
- Use tools like **Whirlpool** (on Samourai), **JoinMarket**, or **Silent Payments** for stronger on-chain privacy. 
### 🧭 STAY HUMBLE. STACK SATS.

- Ignore price noise. Focus on accumulation and long-term sovereignty.
- Bitcoin is for the long haul — not get-rich-quick schemes.
- Help others, but don’t brag. OpSec first, always. 
### 📚 NEVER STOP LEARNING

- Recommended reading: _The Bitcoin Standard_, _Inventing Bitcoin_, _21 Lessons_, _A Progressive’s Case for Bitcoin_.
- Verify everything. Don’t trust influencers, even this flyer. Ask questions. Run code. Test assumptions. 
🟠 _This community values liberty, responsibility, and truth._ If you're here to get rich quick, you're in the wrong room. If you're here to build a future of freedom — welcome.

## 🧨 ADDITIONAL SCAMS & THREATS TO WATCH OUT FOR

### 🪤 **"Investment Platforms" and Yield Products**

- Promises of “guaranteed returns” or “passive Bitcoin income” are red flags.
- Custodial lending platforms (e.g. Celsius, BlockFi, Voyager) **have failed spectacularly** and stole user funds. The lesson is simple: **do not lend your Bitcoin** to anyone.

### 🤖 **AI-Powered Phishing**

- AI-generated voice and video deepfakes are now being used to impersonate trusted people.
- Never act on requests for urgent Bitcoin transfers without verifying identity through multiple channels.

### 🧳 **Social Engineering & In-Person Theft**

- Be cautious of sharing your Bitcoin holdings in public. “Flexing” makes you a target.
- Criminals have physically assaulted Bitcoiners for their seed phrases or devices. **OpSec isn't paranoia — it’s prudence.**

### 🎁 **Fake Airdrops & QR Codes**

- QR code scams are rampant. Scammers replace BTC QR codes with their own address.
- Always double-check the receiving address, especially if scanned or copied from the internet.

### ⚠️ **Malicious Wallets & Open Source Spoofs**

- Scammers clone popular wallet software and inject malware.
- Always verify downloads from the **official site** and check **PGP signatures or hashes** if possible. 
## 🔧 ADVANCED BEST PRACTICES

### 🧬 **Multisig & Collaborative Custody**

- Great for larger balances or shared custody (family, businesses, trusts).
- Use tools like **Sparrow**, **Nunchuk**, **Specter**, or **Casa** (with caution—note their data collection history).

### 📟 **Airgapped Signing Devices**

- For high security, use airgapped signing (e.g. Coldcard + SD card or QR-based flow).
- Keeps private keys off internet-connected devices entirely.

### 🌍 **No KYC Acquisitions**

- Buy via peer-to-peer exchanges (e.g. RoboSats, Bisq, Peach) or earn Bitcoin directly.
- Helps protect your future self from data leaks, blacklists, and state overreach.

### 🧾 **Inheritance Planning**

- Don’t leave your loved ones locked out. Create a plan that balances security and accessibility.
- Include written instructions, hardware wallet backups, and trusted keyholders if using multisig. 
## 🧯 PRACTICAL TIPS FOR GROUP EVENTS

- **Discourage open discussion of personal holdings or wallet types.**
- Encourage “privacy by default”: no photos, no videos, no public tagging without consent.
- **Assign a veteran member to discreetly guide newcomers.** Scammers often target new faces.
