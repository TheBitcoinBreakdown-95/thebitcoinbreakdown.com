Writing 101:
 
1. Simple, not smart  
2. Active, not passive  
3. Write first, edit later  
4. Write like you speak  
5. Appeal to 1 to appeal to many  
6. Vary sentence length for rhythm  
7. 80% of your time on the headline
 
And most importantly:
 
Never stop writing.
