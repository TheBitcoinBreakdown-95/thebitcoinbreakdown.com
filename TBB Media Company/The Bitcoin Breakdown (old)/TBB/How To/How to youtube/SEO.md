Youtube SEO comes in three steps:

1. Keyword research
2. Video optimization
3. Boosting your video and tracking your ranking
 
I assume this is basically plan, optimize, learnings
   

Keyword research:

- Go to a keyword research website
    - h-supertools.com
- Need to get a feel for number of searches per month
    - He says 300K per month is a lot
- Keyword difficulty
    - I should shoot for easy, medium and hard keywords is better for if your channel is bigger
    - Use these keywords for inspiration on video topics, and then own those keywords
        - The more you own the higher you rank and the more you grow
        - Its like a freakin game
    - [https://morningfa.me/](https://morningfa.me/)
        - INVITE ONLY - see how to do this
        - Start at topic, then put in the keyword you found
        - Step 3 is where you find the gateway video
            - This is the video that you need to target to break through to search higher
              
              
            
    
Video optimization

- Plan the SEO BEFORE you make the video
    - Base your video ON the keywords!!
    - Mention the target keyword while you are talking (OMFG!)
        - Probably not _that_ important but it does make a difference
- Create a catchy intro
    - Hook the viewer to watch more
    - Increase the view duration - this helps youtube know that the video is performing well
- How to optimize once you create the video:
    - Need to optimize:
        - The title
        - The description
        - The thumbnail
    - *This is the most important thing on youtube
- Tags
    - Less important but good if people misspell the keyword
- Morningfa.me ALSO has a section for putting in your title, description, tags, hashtags, thumbnail
- **Title:**
    - Target Keyword --\> catchy phrase
    - Optimize for lenght
- **Description:**
    - Mention the keyword for sure in the first paragraph
    - Mention tags within the description too - there's a switch to highlight tags
    - Hashtags - click 'associated hashtags Show' to be recommended hashtags
