Giovanni Ceva  
(Milan, 1647 — Mantua, 1724)
         

Mathematician and engineer.  
He was a teacher in Mantua. His book _De Re Nummaria_ (1711) is one of the first works where mathematics is applied to the study of economics. He stated **Ceva's theorem** , referring to the transversals from a point to the vertices of a triangle.
