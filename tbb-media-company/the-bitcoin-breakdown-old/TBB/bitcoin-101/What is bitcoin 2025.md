Bitcoin supporters see bitcoin as the future of money for a few reasons:

- Bitcoin is programmable, meaning anyone or any Ai can develop an app or payments system on top of bitcoin with no rules or regulations, a robust free market ecosystem and technology stack that can improve over time
- Bitcoin is built to handle a deflationary economy. With technological deflation only increasing, debt based and inflation dependent systems of the past will become untenable
- Bitcoin is secure, meaning it can't be hacked and is made to exist as long as the internet
