**@dr_orlovsky** (Maxim Orlovsky)

Great discovery that takes just three steps:
- logical NAND gate with Bitcoin script
- bit commitments
- taproot tree
and makes universal computing machine with them.

However real-world programs, including things like zk-STARKS, require multiplication and modulo division, absent in Bitcoin script, which for any realistic implementation will not fit into even 2^127 NAND gates (restriction imposed by Bitcoin consensus).

Great computing science excursive but it will take a years (or a soft-fork allowing OP_MUL and other arithmetics to get first practical results out of it.
