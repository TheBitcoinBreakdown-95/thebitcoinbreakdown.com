## Top Bitcoin/Crypto Courses & Certifications (Paid)

**1.[Certified Bitcoin Professional (CBP)](https://cryptoconsortium.org/certifications/CBP)**

**Cost – $95 | Certification – Yes**

As suggested by the name, a CBP is one who knows and understands the ins and outs of the Bitcoin blockchain, Bitcoin transactions, and how the Bitcoin network operates. It is a beginner’s certification with a syllabus of 33 topics followed by an online test of 75 questions which lasts 20 minutes.

**2.[Blockchain Certification Training](https://coinsutra.com/go/Edureka/)**

**Cost – $300 | Certification – Yes**

This is an online blockchain certification training that is brought to you by Edureka. The training lasts for 4 weeks, but you will get lifetime access to their Learning Management System (LMS) if you take this course. Here you will learn about the mechanism of Bitcoin transactions, Ethereum transactions, and how to set up a public/private blockchain environment.

Also, you can expect a strengthening of concepts such as cryptography, cryptocurrency mining, security of cryptocurrencies, and [smart contracts](https://coinsutra.com/smart-contracts/). In the end, you will get an opportunity to work on a live blockchain project.

**3.[Ethereum Developer Course](https://academy.b9lab.com/courses/course-v1:B9lab+ETH-16+2017-09/about)**

**Cost – €1450 | Certification – Yes**

If you are an [Ethereum](https://coinsutra.com/ethereum-beginners-guide/) blockchain enthusiast and also have some development experience, then this course is for you. It is a 9-week, in-depth, online course for 6-10 hours per week where you will learn all about Ethereum’s basics, [DApps](https://coinsutra.com/dapps-decentralized-applications/), smart contracts, Solidity, Web3, and Truffle. It will also teach you the DApps back-end and full front-end development through three blockchain-based projects.

At the end of the program, participants are judged based on a certification examination where they are required to score a minimum of 80% to receive an Ethereum blockchain verified certificate.

**4.[Blockchain for Technical Executives and Analysts](https://academy.b9lab.com/courses/course-v1:B9lab+CTO-6+2017-08/about)**

**Cost – €2350 | Certification – Yes**

Some of you might be thinking that these courses are only for developers, but that’s not true. Blockchain and cryptocurrencies are also for people who are not programmers. This course is designed for people who are technical executives, analysts, and consultants. Here you will learn everything you need to know about the blockchain, smart contracts, and DApps. Also, expect thorough coverage of Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Hyperledger (public/private blockchain) from both the technical as well as the social perspective.

At the end of the program, participants are judged based on a certification examination where they are required to score a minimum of 80% to receive an Ethereum blockchain verified certificate.

**5.[Certified Blockchain Professional (CBCP)](https://blockchaininstituteoftechnology.com/certifications/certified-blockchain-professional/)**[TM](https://blockchaininstituteoftechnology.com/certifications/certified-blockchain-professional/)

**Cost – $275 | Certification – Yes**

If you are already a blockchain and cryptocurrency expert, then this is for you. This is not a course but a certification exam that will help you prove your professional expertise in this area and will set you apart from other blockchain and cryptocurrency enthusiasts. If you want to level up and show your expertise to the world in a professional way, then this the way to go.

The CBCP examination consists of 90 questions which must be answered in 60 minutes. Upon successful completion of the examination and achieving a passing grade of 75% or above on all test sections, you get CBCP certification from the _Blockchain Institute of Technology_ which is then embedded on the blockchain.
