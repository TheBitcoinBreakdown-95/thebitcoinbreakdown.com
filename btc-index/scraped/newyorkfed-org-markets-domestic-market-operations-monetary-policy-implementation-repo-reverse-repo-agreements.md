    * Repo and Reverse Repo Agreements The New York Fed’s Open Market Trading Desk (the Desk) is [authorized and directed by the Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC)](https://www.federalreserve.gov/monetarypolicy/files/FOMC_AuthorizationsContinuingDirectivesOMOs.pdf) to conduct repurchase agreement (repo) and reverse repo transactions. These open market operations support monetary policy implementation and smooth market functioning, by helping maintain the federal funds (fed funds) rate within the FOMC’s target range. Repos are a common secured money market transaction where the Desk purchases securities from a counterparty subject to an agreement to resell the securities at a later date.
