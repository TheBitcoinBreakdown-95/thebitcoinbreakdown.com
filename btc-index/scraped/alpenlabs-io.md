## Free users from centralized BTC

Apps on Alpen free users from custodial BTC finance and honest-majority BTC bridges through Alpen's novel _1-of-N Bitcoin bridge_ that drastically improves safety.

## Build any EVM app imaginable

Alpen opens the gates to all possible financial apps for BTC with an EVM-compatible environment. Innovate endlessly with support from the largest developer ecosystem in the industry.  


## Settle transactions directly on Bitcoin

Alpen settles all of its financial transactions directly on Bitcoin through its unique ZK rollup with optimistic bridge architecture. All transactions on Alpen inherit Bitcoin's double-spend security.
