U.S. number of government employees 1982-2023  Published by [Statista Research Department](/aboutus/our-research-commitment),  Nov 28, 2025 In 2023, around 19.58 million people were working for state and local governments in the United States. This is much higher than the number of federal government (civilian) employees, which stood at about 2.87 million people in that year.
