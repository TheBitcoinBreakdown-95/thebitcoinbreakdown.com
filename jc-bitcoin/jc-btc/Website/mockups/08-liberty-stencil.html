About

The Meetup

Jersey City Bitcoin started in 2022 as a handful of friends who wanted to learn together. Today we're 100+ members meeting monthly at Zeppelin Hall -- the beer garden with the big parking lot, the long community tables, and a 5-minute walk from the Liberty State Park PATH station.

We meet in the shadow of the Statue of Liberty -- the symbol that welcomed millions to a new world. We think Bitcoin is the next chapter of that story: financial freedom, self-sovereignty, and the right to transact without permission.

Two formats. Come to one, come to both. The only requirement is curiosity.
