About

Education Without Hype

Jersey City Bitcoin started in 2022 as a handful of friends who wanted to learn together. Today we're 100+ members meeting monthly at Zeppelin Hall -- the beer garden with the big parking lot and the easy PATH access.

We run two formats: Socratic Seminars for deep-dives into protocol, economics, and philosophy, and Bitcoin & Beer for casual conversation and community. Whether you've been stacking since 2013 or you just heard about this thing last week, you're welcome here.
