Jersey City Bitcoin started in 2022 as a few friends who wanted to learn together. Today we're 100+ members meeting monthly at Zeppelin Hall -- the beer garden with the big parking lot and the easy PATH access from NYC.

We believe Bitcoin education should be free, accessible, and free from hype. No price predictions, no trading signals, no shilling. Just honest conversation about sound money.

The schlep matters. We picked a spot that's easy to reach from everywhere -- NJ Transit, PATH from Manhattan, or just drive and park for free. No excuses not to show up.
