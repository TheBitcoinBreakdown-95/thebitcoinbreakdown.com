About

The Blueprint

Jersey City Bitcoin started in 2022 as a handful of friends who wanted to understand Bitcoin together. Today we're 100+ members meeting monthly at Zeppelin Hall -- the beer garden with the parking lot, the PATH access, and the long community tables.

We run two formats: Socratic Seminars for structured protocol discussions, and Bitcoin & Beer for casual community. Whether you've been stacking since 2013 or you just heard about this thing last week, you're welcome.

No price predictions. No trading signals. No shilling. Just honest education about the most important monetary technology of our time.
