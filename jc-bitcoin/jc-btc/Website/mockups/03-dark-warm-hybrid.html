About

Education Without the Noise

Jersey City Bitcoin started in 2022 as a few friends who wanted to learn together. Today we're 100+ members strong, meeting monthly at Zeppelin Hall -- the beer garden with the big parking lot and easy PATH access from NYC.

We run two formats. Come to one, come to both.

Socratic Seminar

Structured discussion on protocol development, economics, and philosophy. Bring questions, leave with understanding.

Bitcoin & Beer

Unstructured hangout. Meet other bitcoiners, share stories, ask anything. The only agenda is good conversation.
