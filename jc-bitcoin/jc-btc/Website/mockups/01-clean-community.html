About Us

Bitcoin Education for Everyone

Jersey City Bitcoin started in 2022 as a small group of friends who wanted to learn together. Today we're 100+ members strong, meeting monthly at Zeppelin Hall in Jersey City.

We believe Bitcoin education should be free, accessible, and free from hype. No price predictions, no trading signals, no shilling -- just honest conversation about the most important monetary technology of our time.
