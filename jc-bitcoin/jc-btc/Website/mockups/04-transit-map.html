The JCB Line

First Stop

Show Up

Second Thursday of every month at Zeppelin Hall -- the beer garden with free parking and PATH access from NYC. Walk in, grab a drink, find us.

Learn

Socratic Seminars

Structured deep-dives into Bitcoin protocol, economics, and philosophy. We discuss recent developments under Chatham House rules. Bring questions.

Connect

Bitcoin & Beer

No agenda, no slides, no pressure. Just bitcoiners at all levels talking over good food and cold drinks. This is where friendships form.

Build

Workshops & Demos

Hands-on technical training. Wallet setup, node running, Lightning payments, Nostr. We learn by doing.

Destination

Sovereignty

The goal isn't to sell you on Bitcoin. It's to give you the knowledge to decide for yourself. We don't predict the price. We study the protocol.
