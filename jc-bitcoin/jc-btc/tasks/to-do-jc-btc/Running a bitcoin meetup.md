- Bitcoin audible, poe's law comes into full flower
	- if you basically lose the ability to trust anything, you cannot see, any text or information about the world, any photo, any video, disembodied voice, zoom face or phone call, any of it, what do you do?
	- you stop trusting it.
	- the world gets small again. you trust what you see with your own eyes. business moves to highly protected, verified networks...
	- so for the real high trust? face to face. IRL. hands get shaken, eyes get looked into, and people once more do business directly with people.
	- i think this has the potential to make people happier.
	- we’re social animals. we want to be around one another. we want face time not facetime. we want contact, lunch, talking and body language not the half communication of voice and and video calls and the quarter communication of text.
- What does this mean for bitcoin meetups?
	- We're building webs of trust, before ai fundamentally undermines our ability to trust our online interactions.

- Why bitcoin meetups are so important
    - Makes bitcoin feel "real"
        - Because most people don't understand how much of a paradigm shift bitcoin is, it becomes hard to talk to regular people, and you feel a bit crazy.
        - Meeting other people who think the same way as you is a gamechanger,
    - Third space - the idea of having somewhere besides work and home to connect with peers,
    - Meetups create spaces for action
        - Combine technical expertise to help answer community questions, onboard businesses, and host workshops/socratic seminars as a way to hone education skills and provide value to the community
    - We have this amazing technology, the internet, to coordinate over the entire world, and this has been instrumental for the bitcoin revolution, but we're still human, we still need that in person interaction
    - Issues like OP_return can't be solved over an inflammatory medium like Twitter
    - Meetups Create Trust
        - Meetup organizers play a uniquely powerful role in Bitcoin adoption. When people show up regularly, form real friendships, and share personal stories, the trust generated is profound.
        - local meetup leaders are some of the most important — and often overlooked — educators and evangelists in Bitcoin today.
    - Meetups are a trusted place to serve the community
        - With so much noise, shitcoins, scams, and bad actors in the space it's vital that we have a healthy network of trustworthy educators
      
    
 I think back to enlightenment salon's, and the american revolution,

    - Salon culture played a significant role in spreading Enlightenment ideas
	    - venue where intellectuals could gather to discuss innovative concepts related to reason, liberty, and equality
    - They played a critical role in shaping public opinion and providing an intellectual foundation for revolutionary events, such as the Declaration of Independence and the French Revolution.
        - Many of the American Revolution’s critical documents – such as the Declaration of Independence, the Virginia Declaration of Rights and the United States Constitution – were studied and discussed in the salons of Paris. 
        - American diplomats and visitors like Benjamin Franklin and Thomas Jefferson were warmly welcomed in the more prestigious salons.
    - These salons were crucial cultural institutions that provided a space for open debate and the dissemination of radical thoughts outside of formal institutions, contributing to major shifts in public opinion and revolutionary movements

Our mission is to create a Bitcoin education center in Jersey City,
- empowering individuals with financial sovereignty and combating centralized control of money through grassroots activism.
- We aim to build locally, educate our community, train educators, and open source our best practices to streamline local adoption all over the world.
- By inspiring curiosity, we will bridge our community to bitcoin infrastructure
- Activism is crucial as it helps us fight against convenience and centralized control while empowering individuals with knowledge and skills necessary in the face of emerging CBDCs and state power struggles.
