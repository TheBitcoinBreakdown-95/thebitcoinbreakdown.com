### Market Mechanics: Reflexivity

29. "A key way Bitcoin is different than equities: reflexivity." (HHorsley / Twitter)
30. When a stock goes up, the multiple goes up, and at some point people say "this is overvalued" and lose interest until the price falls. (HHorsley)
31. Bitcoin is different: when Bitcoin's price goes up, people view it as more likely to succeed and therefore more valuable -- it's likely to go up even further. (HHorsley)
32. "A powerful dynamic." (HHorsley)
