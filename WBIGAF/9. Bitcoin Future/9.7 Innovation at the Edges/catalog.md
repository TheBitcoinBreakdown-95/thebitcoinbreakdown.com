### The Dumb Network Thesis

1. Bitcoin enables innovation at the edges via five properties: dumb network, open source, permissionless, global, and programmable. (Author)
2. When everyone has access to an open market, ideas flourish. (Author)
3. "Simplicity at the center allows for complexity at the edge, which fosters the vast decentralization of services." (FEE / fee.org)
4. "Smart" networks offer sophisticated services that require simple end-user devices; "dumb" networks offer basic service and require intelligent end-user devices -- dumb networks push innovation to the edge, giving end-users control over pace and direction of innovation. (FEE / fee.org)
