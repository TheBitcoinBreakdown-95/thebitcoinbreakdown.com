### Bitcoin Is the Perfect Money for AI Agents (Alby Blog / External Source)

19. There are at least three requirements for money used by an AI agent: permissionless, instant settlement, and micropayment capability. (Alby Blog)
20. AI agents aren't people and don't have identities -- they need permissionless money. (Alby Blog)
21. AI agents need to instantly allocate resources without the risk of chargeback. (Alby Blog)
22. AI agents need to send tiny fractions of a cent in real time -- streaming money. (Alby Blog)
23. Bitcoin is permissionless -- a bearer asset requiring no identity, so AI agents can autonomously send and receive it. (Alby Blog)
