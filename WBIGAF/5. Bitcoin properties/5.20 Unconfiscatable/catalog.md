9. The way around this is through complex custody solutions where you and a few other parties hold keys and all must agree to send money in a multi-signature arrangement. (Author)
10. You could keep your keys in a dummy wallet or put them in another country as additional protection. (Author)

### Infeasibility of Mass State Confiscation

11. The feasibility to enforce confiscation of millions of people's bitcoin is untenable -- it would be extremely difficult from a logistical standpoint. (Author)
12. Mass bitcoin confiscation would be politically unpopular, require advanced totalitarianism, and require the manpower and resources to execute. (Author)
13. Mass confiscation would be met with armed resistance. (Author)
14. If done on a mass scale, it would encourage the populace to adopt complex custody solutions to make physical confiscation impossible. (Author)
