### Core Programmability Arguments

2. "It is dynamic, upgradable and extendable. It does not need throwing out and replacing with each new iteration, it will continuously improve." (Neil Woodfine)

### Protocol Capabilities

5. Bitcoin Script allows for complex transaction types, timelocks, and multisignature schemes. (Author)
6. Soft forks like SegWit and Taproot have increased the programmatic capabilities on the base chain. (Author)
7. SegWit and Taproot have unlocked new scaling capabilities including the Lightning Network, Taptrees, BitVM, and DLCs. (Author)
8. Further updates like covenants and ZK op codes could allow for greater upgrades including shared UTXO schemes and trustless bridges to higher layers. (Author)
9. These upgrades could give bitcoin unlimited programmatic capabilities, unlimited scaling potential, and the full range of financial services offered in the current system. (Author)
10. The entire crypto ecosystem's programmatic potential could eventually be implemented on bitcoin's stack. (Author)
