### Multiple Transmission Methods

9. There are many private ways to send a bitcoin transaction -- since it is all just information, you can send it within an image or a song, broadcast it via radio, call a phone service to decrypt, send it over satellite, or use an app on your phone. (Author)

10. Bitcoin makes it possible to secure and transport your wealth without anyone's permission. (Author)

### Vijay Boyapati -- Permissionless at Network Level

11. The key attribute making Bitcoin valuable for illicit activities is that it is "permissionless" at the network level -- when bitcoins are transmitted, there is no human intervention deciding whether the transaction should be allowed. (Vijay Boyapati)
12. As a distributed peer-to-peer network, Bitcoin is by its very nature designed to be censorship-resistant. (Vijay Boyapati)
