### Bitcoin as Asset vs. Medium of Exchange

12. Governments will never give up their currency and regulated markets, so Bitcoin will probably not be a primary medium of exchange but will instead primarily be an asset. (Author's notes)
13. Because Bitcoin can serve both functions, the medium-of-exchange aspect can be reserved for people and industries that need it outside the regulated system. (Author's notes)
14. Adoption of Bitcoin as an asset by the regulated economy will act as a stabilizing force for the value of Bitcoin -- volatility goes down as less supply is actually trading. (Author's notes)
15. The store-of-value aspect of Bitcoin keeps it as a stronger freedom tool for those who need it most -- the store-of-value buyers are essentially subsidizing the freedom money function. (Author's notes)
