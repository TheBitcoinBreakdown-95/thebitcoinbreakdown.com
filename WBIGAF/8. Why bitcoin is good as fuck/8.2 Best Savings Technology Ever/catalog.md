### Bitcoin as Superior Collateral (Bitcoin Magazine)

1. Bitcoin is an extremely liquid asset -- traded 24/7, no weekends, no banking holidays, with massive liquidity pools across a variety of fiat currencies available globally. (Bitcoin Magazine)
2. Bitcoin's liquidity allows for instant conversion to fiat upon borrower liquidation or loan repayment -- a serious competitive advantage for lenders. (Bitcoin Magazine)
3. Bitcoin may be the only loan collateral which can be instantly and dynamically hedged. (Bitcoin Magazine)
4. Bitcoin enables the creation of programmable lending products and ownership mechanisms -- solving the trusted-third-party problem via non-custodial lending. (Bitcoin Magazine)
5. Bitcoin allows distribution of collateral claims or conditional logic for redemption, automatically executed by the Bitcoin network rather than a centralized institution. (Bitcoin Magazine)
6. There will only be 21 million bitcoin -- collateral gets more valuable over time, reducing borrower incentive to sell and attracting more willing lenders. (Bitcoin Magazine)
7. Bitcoin allows selective transparency of assets when useful, while allowing complete anonymity when desired -- e.g., proving collateral ownership to a lender. (Bitcoin Magazine)
8. Bitcoin is sovereign -- you have keys to your bitcoin just like keys to your house or car. Unlike real estate or car collateral, you can conditionally own bitcoin during the lending agreement. (Bitcoin Magazine)
9. Bitcoin is protected cryptographically, economically, and socially -- ownership can be distributed between multiple independent parties with offline wallets and various security methods. (Bitcoin Magazine)
10. Bitcoin is market-driven -- price reflects the market almost instantly, is not determined by any individual, and is extremely difficult to manipulate. (Bitcoin Magazine)
11. Bitcoin costs almost the same in fiat in any part of the world, determined by a global market. (Bitcoin Magazine)
12. Bitcoin's blockchain allows tracking of collateral address in real time -- any price fluctuation can be reacted to appropriately, with no market closures. (Bitcoin Magazine)
13. Bitcoin is objective and honest -- price cannot be determined by personal views or forecasting. To borrow against bitcoin, you only need to have bitcoin; credit history and social score are irrelevant. (Bitcoin Magazine)
